_CTITLE_Places_RESET_
 _CPLACE_Foundations_RESET_ - Four stacks in the top left corner
 _CPLACE_Hand_RESET_ - Column of cards in the top right corner
 _CPLACE_Stock_RESET_ - Pile of cards next to the hand
 _CPLACE_Waste_RESET_ - Pile of cards next to the stock
 _CPLACE_Tableau_RESET_ - Seven columns in the second row of cards

_CTITLE_Goal_RESET_
 In solitaire, your goal is to put all cards into their foundations.

_CTITLE_Scoring_RESET_
 _GREEN_10_RESET_ points for each card moved to a foundation stack.
 _GREEN_5_RESET_ points for each card moved from the waste to a tableau stack.
 _GREEN_5_RESET_ points for each card turned face-up in a tableau stack.
 _GREEN_3_RESET_ points for each card moved from one tableau stack to another.
 _RED_-2_RESET_ points for each 10 seconds elapsed during a timed game. (Evaluated after completion)
 _RED_-15_RESET_ points for each card moved from a foundation stack to a tableau stack.
 _RED_-20_RESET_ points for each pass through the stock after four passes (Draw Three option).
 _RED_-100_RESET_ points for each pass through the stock after one pass (Draw One option).
 You receive a _GREEN_bonus_RESET_ when you complete a timed game. The shorter the game, the larger the bonus.
